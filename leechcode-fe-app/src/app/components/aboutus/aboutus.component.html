Overview

Over the last 10 years the rise of programing challenges from companies like LeetCode and HackerRank has revolutionized the way software engineers are hired. With these new platforms, engineers are expected to be proficient in solving a series of coding challenges in a short amount of time. Leechcode is a free online coding challenge platform made for companies to test and evaluate potential candidates.
